How to launch Google Chrome in Kiosk Mode for POS Auto-Print:

1. Create a shortcut to Chrome.
2. Right-click the shortcut → Properties → Target:

   "C:\Program Files\Google\Chrome\Application\chrome.exe" --kiosk-printing --kiosk https://your-odoo-url

Example:
"C:\Program Files\Google\Chrome\Application\chrome.exe" --kiosk-printing --kiosk https://mypos.example.com/web

3. This will open directly into Odoo POS screen, and any window.print() will auto print without asking.

Enjoy auto-printing POS reports!
